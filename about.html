About

I’m a digital media designer specialising in UX/UI and web design, with a passion for creating accessible and intuitive experiences. Based in Melbourne, I have recently completed my Bachelor of Design (Digital Media) at RMIT University.

With over 5 years of experience in the arts industry, I bring a creative, user-centred approach to my work, designing solutions that are both visually compelling and meaningful for diverse audiences.


Through my customer-facing roles, I’ve gained insight into the challenges people face when interacting with systems and technology. This has strengthened my empathy for users and my commitment to creating seamless, accessible digital experiences in an increasingly online world.
